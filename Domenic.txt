'''Domenic Aquilina, Malta – CV'''

Born on August 2<sup>nd, </sup>1957 in Senglea, Malta, Domenic has been involved in the photojournalism sphere since his early twenties. 

Senior official Malta football writer / photojournalist correspondent to [http://www.uefa.com UEFA.com] – the official site of the Union of European Football Associations - for 23 years to date, and a regular photographic contributor to various UEFA online and published contributions

A self-taught seasoned all round photographer, Domenic’s association with UEFA spans a long number of years.&nbsp; Specializing in photo reportage, the Malta-based photographer has covered 18 UEFA Champions League finals to date, plus numerous UEFA Champions League matches and other UEFA official matches. His photojournalistic contributions to UEFA.com and various UEFA publications have been numerous and still ongoing.

Accredited member of the Association Internationalle de la Presse Sportive (A.I.P.S), Institute of Maltese Journalists, The Malta Sportswriters Association, The Malta Institute of Professional Photographers and the International Federation of Journalists.

Occupied the role of official photographer within the Malta Football Association and as official photographer for the Malta Senior National Football team as from September 2010 to November 2023








Awarded with the SWPP UK "Sport General Photographer of The Year 2011" award at the SWPP Convention 2012 in London, England.

Judged as “Malta Sports Photographer of the Year” at the Malta Journalistic Awards in 2014, 2017, 2018, 2019, 2020 and 2023

In January 2023, the Malta based sports photographer launched his personal autobiography “The Malta Falcon”. Domenic donated the proceedings to Hospice Malta, an NGO which provides the highest standards of Palliative care on the island free of charge.
